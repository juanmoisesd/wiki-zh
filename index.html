拉丁美洲的阿尔茨海默症

阿尔茨海默病和其他痴呆症是拉丁美洲增长最快的公共卫生挑战之一。该地区的人口转变——以快速人口老龄化为特征——正在加速神经退行性疾病的负担，卫生系统尚未做好应对准备。

📚 研究现状

目前估计拉丁美洲有400-500万痴呆症患者，由于人口老龄化，预计到2050年这一数字将增加两倍。该地区面临独特挑战：教育水平较低（已知保护因素）、血管危险因素高发、专科医生稀缺，以及延误诊断的重大污名化问题。泛美卫生组织估计，该地区不到10%的痴呆症病例获得正式诊断。

📊 我们数据的主要发现

  • 患病率：拉丁美洲65岁以上人群中占6-8%（2020-2023）
  • 专科医生密度：每10万居民0.5-2.5名神经科医生
  • 照护者负担：85-90%的护理由非正式（无薪）家庭照护者提供
  • 经济成本：估计每年150-250亿美元

🏛️ 政策含义

NCRA数据集的证据表明，老龄化指数较高且心理健康专科医生密度较低的国家面临复合风险。优先干预措施包括：扩大初级医疗痴呆症检测能力，以及制定与世卫组织《2017-2025年痴呆症全球行动计划》一致的国家痴呆症计划。

Mental Health in Latin America

Mental health disorders affect approximately 100 million people in Latin America — about 17% of the population. Despite this burden, the treatment gap (percentage of people not receiving care) exceeds 70% in most countries, driven by shortage of specialists, stigma, and insufficient public funding.

📚 研究现状

The region has made progress in developing national mental health policies, but implementation gaps remain large. Psychiatric bed density averages 2-3 per 100,000 — far below WHO recommendations of 10-15. The COVID-19 pandemic increased prevalence of depression and anxiety disorders by 25-30% in the region according to WHO estimates.

📊 我们数据的主要发现

  • Treatment gap: 70-85% across Latin American countries
  • Mental health budget: <2% of total health budget in most countries
  • Psychiatrists: 0.5-3 per 100,000 (vs. WHO recommended 3+)
  • Depression prevalence: 5-8% of adult population

🏛️ 政策含义

The NCRA mental health specialist dataset reveals stark inequalities between urban and rural areas and between countries. Chile, Argentina, and Uruguay show specialist densities 3-5x higher than Bolivia, Paraguay, and Honduras. This data directly informs PAHO's Mental Health Action Plan implementation at country level.
